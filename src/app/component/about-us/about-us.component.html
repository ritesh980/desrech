About Us

Desreach is India's first Eco-System platform for art and design lovers. It is established with the provision of changing the world with art and designs. We offer independent artists and designers a platform as well as a new way to sell their creations, learn from the professionals, teach emerging and aspiring artists and designers through meetup and workshops.
